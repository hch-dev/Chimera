Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

What is Chimera and how does it work?

Chimera is an AI-powered phishing detection and prevention tool that analyzes URLs and emails for malicious content. Our system uses machine learning algorithms to identify suspicious patterns, check against threat databases, and provide risk assessments to help you stay safe online.

Is Chimera free to use?

Yes, Chimera offers free basic scanning for URLs and emails. We also provide premium features for organizations and advanced users who need additional capabilities like API access, custom integrations, and detailed analytics.

How accurate is the phishing detection?

Our AI models achieve over 95% accuracy in detecting phishing attempts. However, no system is perfect, and we continuously improve our algorithms based on new threats and user feedback. Always exercise caution when dealing with suspicious content.

What types of URLs can I scan?

You can scan any HTTP or HTTPS URL. Our scanner analyzes domains, checks SSL certificates, evaluates content patterns, and cross-references with known threat databases. We recommend scanning any suspicious links before clicking them.

How long does a scan take?

Most scans complete within 2-5 seconds. The time varies based on the complexity of the URL and the number of security checks needed. We optimize for speed while maintaining thorough analysis.

Can I scan multiple URLs at once?

Currently, our free version supports one URL at a time. Premium users can access batch scanning capabilities through our API or dashboard, allowing multiple simultaneous scans.

Is my data safe when using Chimera?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security. Scanned URLs and emails are processed securely, and we don't store personal information without consent. Our systems use encryption and follow strict data protection protocols.

What happens if I find a false positive?

We continuously work to minimize false positives. If you encounter one, please report it through our feedback system. Your reports help us improve our algorithms and provide more accurate results for everyone.

What browsers are supported?

Chimera works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version for the best experience and security.

Do you offer a mobile app?

Our website is fully mobile-optimized and works great on smartphones and tablets. We're also developing native mobile apps for iOS and Android, which will be available soon.
